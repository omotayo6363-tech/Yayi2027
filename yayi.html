📋 YAYI 2027 MANIFESTO - OGUN FORWARD
🏗 Infrastructure and Roads ▼
Complete all abandoned road projects across Ogun State. Construct new dual carriageways linking Abeokuta, Sagamu, and Ilaro. Partner with private sector for road development. Bridge the infrastructure gap between Ogun East, Central and Yewa zones.
🏥 Healthcare for All ▼
Build new General Hospitals across all senatorial zones. Equip all Primary Health Centres with modern facilities. Free healthcare for pregnant women, children under 5, and the elderly. Launch YAYI Health Insurance for all civil servants.
📚 Education Revolution ▼
Renovate and equip all public primary and secondary schools. Free school meals programme for all public school pupils. Digital skills training centres in all 20 LGAs. Increase bursary and scholarship grants significantly.
💼 Economy and Jobs ▼
Create hundreds of thousands of direct and indirect jobs. Support SMEs with grants and low-interest loans. Develop agriculture value chain - Ogun becomes food basket. Attract major foreign direct investment through diaspora network.
💡 Power and Energy ▼
Dedicated power supply partnerships for Ogun State. Solar mini-grids for all rural communities. Street lighting covering all 236 wards. Renewable energy subsidies for manufacturers and businesses.
🌾 Agriculture and Food Security ▼
Farm-to-Market programme linking 100,000 farmers to markets. Modern irrigation for dry-season farming. Youth agribusiness incubator empowering young farmers. Zero post-harvest loss target through cold-chain logistics.
