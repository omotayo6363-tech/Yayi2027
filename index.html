🇳🇬 Our Manifesto

Senator YAYI's vision for Ogun State — tap each pillar to read the plan

🏗️
Infrastructure & Roads
Priority 1
›

Complete transformation of Ogun State's road network, with special focus on long-neglected Ogun West.

  • Repair and expand all major roads in Yewa/Awori
  • New bridges connecting communities
  • Rural road connectivity for farmers
  • Modern drainage systems to stop flooding
🎓
Education & Youth
Priority 2
›

World-class education for every child, with strong investment in technical and vocational training.

  • Free quality education from primary to secondary
  • New schools in underserved communities
  • Tech hubs and coding academies for youth
  • Scholarship fund for Ogun State students
🌾
Agriculture & Economy
Priority 3
›

Harness Ogun State's enormous agricultural potential to create wealth and jobs for all citizens.

  • Modern farming equipment for cooperatives
  • Agro-processing zones to add value locally
  • Export programme for cassava, palm oil, yam
  • Microfinance for small-scale farmers
🏥
Healthcare
Priority 4
›

Quality healthcare available to every citizen across all 20 LGAs.

  • Upgrade all General Hospitals across the state
  • Free maternal and child health services
  • Community health outreach in rural areas
  • Attract doctors and specialists to Ogun
⚡
Power & Technology
Priority 5
›

Stable power and digital infrastructure to attract investment and transform daily life.

  • Solar micro-grids for all communities
  • Broadband internet across the state
  • Smart governance — digital state services
  • Industrial parks powered by clean energy
⚖️
Equity — YEWA LO KAN
Core Promise
›

YEWA LO KAN — The Yewa turn has come. After decades of neglect, Ogun West will finally get its fair share.

  • Equal project allocation across all zones
  • Yewa/Awori people in key government positions
  • Dedicated development fund for Ogun West
  • Full representation for all Ogun communities
