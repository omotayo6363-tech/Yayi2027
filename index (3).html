📋 YAYI's 6-Point Agenda for Ogun State
Ti YAYI Yato Si Ti Awon Oselu Alagidi
🌾Agriculture & Food Security▼
Modernise farming across all 20 LGAs with irrigation, storage, and market access. Support smallholder farmers with inputs, training, and cooperatives. Make Ogun the food basket of South-West Nigeria.
🏥Healthcare for All▼
Upgrade general hospitals in all senatorial districts. Ensure free maternal and child healthcare. Deploy mobile health clinics to reach rural communities in Yewa and beyond.
📚Education & Youth Empowerment▼
Rebuild public schools, pay teachers promptly, and launch the YAYI Scholarship Fund for tertiary students. Establish tech and vocational hubs so Ogun youth can compete globally.
🛣️Infrastructure & Roads▼
Complete all abandoned road projects. Tar rural roads connecting farming communities to markets. Construct bridges and improve drainage to end flooding in urban Ogun communities.
💼Economy & Job Creation▼
Attract foreign investment with a dedicated Ogun Investment Office. Create 100,000 new jobs in manufacturing, agriculture, and technology within the first two years. Support SMEs with low-interest state loans.
⚡Power & Clean Energy▼
Partner with solar companies to bring renewable energy to schools, hospitals, and markets. Negotiate reliable power supply and reduce dependence on the national grid across all 20 LGAs.
