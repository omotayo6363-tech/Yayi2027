📋 About Senator YAYI

Senator Solomon Olamilekan Adeola CON, FCA "YAYI" is the APC Governorship Candidate for Ogun State in the 2027 elections. A distinguished statesman, accomplished chartered accountant, and seasoned legislator with a proven record of integrity, excellence, and service to the people of Ogun State.

🏆 Accolades & Achievements

  • 🎖️ Senator of the Federal Republic of Nigeria — Ogun West Senatorial District
  • 🎖️ Fellow, Institute of Chartered Accountants of Nigeria (FCA)
  • 🎖️ National Honours Award — Commander of the Order of the Niger (CON)
  • 🎖️ Chairman, Senate Committee on Finance
  • 🎖️ Chairman, Senate Committee on Appropriations
  • 🎖️ Former Lagos State House of Assembly Member
  • 🎖️ Former Lagos State Commissioner for Finance
  • 🎖️ Advocate for fiscal transparency and accountability in governance
  • 🎖️ Champion of grassroots development across all 20 LGAs of Ogun State
  • 🎖️ APC Governorship Candidate — Ogun State 2027

🎯 Our Vision for Ogun State

A prosperous, equitable, and secure Ogun State where every citizen has access to quality education, healthcare, infrastructure, and economic opportunities.

🏗️ Infrastructure & Roads

  • Complete all abandoned road projects
  • Build new bridges connecting Ogun West
  • Expand rural electrification
  • Modern water supply for all LGAs

🎓 Education & Youth

  • Free and quality public education
  • Scholarship programmes for Ogun students
  • Tech hubs and skills acquisition centres
  • Youth entrepreneurship fund

🏥 Healthcare for All

  • Renovate and equip all general hospitals
  • Free maternal and child healthcare
  • Community health centres in every ward
  • Health insurance scheme for the poor

🌾 Agriculture & Economy

  • Empower farmers with subsidised inputs
  • Agro-processing zones to add value locally
  • Attract foreign and local investment
  • Create 100,000 jobs in first term

🔒 Security & Peace

  • Equip and support security agencies
  • Community policing initiative
  • Peace and dialogue across all communities
